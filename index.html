About This Project

Originally conceived as a set of 4 prints for our own wall during the summer of 2020, Ryan started creating these renderings as a graphic tribute to the incredible beauty and history of neighborhood cityscapes and our visual connections with these locations. We especially love the craftsmanship and attention to detail common in the era at the turn of the twentieth century. All items are crafted & printed on-site at our studio in the Lake View East neighborhood of Chicago.
