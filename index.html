About This Project

A passion project by VagabondStudiosDesign.com

Originally conceived as a small set of prints for our own wall, Ryan started creating these renderings as a graphic tribute to the incredible beauty and history of neighborhood streetscapes and our visual connections with these locations. We especially love the craftsmanship and attention to detail common in the era at the turn of the twentieth century. Every print is delivered on high quality white felt card stock sourced from Neenah paper company in Wisconsin, and all items are printed on-site at our studio in the Lake View East neighborhood of Chicago.
