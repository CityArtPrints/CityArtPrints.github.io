Moore House

Oak Park IL
Approached by the Frank Lloyd Wright Trust in connection with a fundraising event, this rendering was created of the beautiful Moore House in Oak Park, Illinois. While Tudor Revival is not the typical style of the architect, this particular home fits in perfectly with our graphic style and aesthetic taste in homes of the late 19th century.
