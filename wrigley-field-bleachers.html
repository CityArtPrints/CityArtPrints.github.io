Wrigley Field

(Bleachers / Scoreboard)
Lake View - Chicago IL
Most ballparks immortalize their greatest athletes at the gates, but the entry at Sheffield and Waveland is different. The voice of Harry Caray transmitted the perfect blend of fandom, humor, and community into our home televisions that made him revered by cubs fans in a way players rarely were, and the bleachers embody this type of spirit. Moreover, it's impossible to render this vantage point without including the infamous 'W' flag, as it showcases an essential emotional and connective history with commuters, fans, and the neighborhood.
