Webster Hall

East Village - Manhattan NY
This historic "assembly hall for hire" in the East Village likely contains more ballroom history than any other venue in the country. Opened in 1886, the space has hosted (and recorded) countless notable music events from all genres, in addition to bohemian masquerade balls, society dinners, infamous labor & political rallies, and any other event you can imagine. Despite being plagued by five major fires in her lifetime, this old world concert hall has survived and continues to thrive as a top venue for live shows and a critical part of the New York music scene.
