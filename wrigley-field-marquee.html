Wrigley Field

Lake View - Chicago IL
Hearing the iconic Lowrey organ echo into the stadium and streets that surround this ballpark is an essential part of summer on the north side. Firmly embedded within the neighborhood, Wrigley Field is a living museum of baseball history unlike any other. Ivy covered outfield walls, a hand operated scoreboard, and recent modern amenities with respect for the ballparks past make 'The Friendly Confines' a quintessential Chicago experience.
