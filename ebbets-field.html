Ebbets Field

Crown Heights - Brooklyn NY
The power of baseball nostalgia and Brooklyn past can be embodied in the memories and enduring legacy of Ebbets Field. The ball park was designed with a grand entrance rotunda including intricate marble detailing and even a baseball themed chandelier. From the first night game in 1938 to Jackie Robinson's culture shifting moment of 1947, this stadium lives on in our psyche despite its demolition in 1960.
